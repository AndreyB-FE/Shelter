True Heart's mission

The mission of True Heart is to advance and support the practice of shelter medicine in order to improve community animal health and well-being.

Guidelines for Standards of Care in Animal Shelters

True Heart is an international organization whose mission is to improve the health and well-being of animals in shelters through the advancement of shelter medicine. This document is the result of work that True Heart began in 2008 to address the lack of guidelines or standards of care for animals in shelters.
